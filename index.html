KNOW MORE

We start with a sketch, which is basically just a scribble of an idea. It's our way of getting your brain going on what we can do together.

After sketching, we refine the lines to create a structured outline, defining the shape and details of the artwork.

We add color to bring the artwork to life. This step involves choosing the right palette and shading techniques.

The final step involves refining all elements, adding lighting effects, and final touch-ups to complete the artwork.
